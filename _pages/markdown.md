## Cylindrical Waves 

An so we have the wave equation in cylindrical coordinates:

$$
\displaylines{
\frac{\partial^{2}u_{r}}{\partial r^{2}}+\frac{\partial}{\partial r}\left(\frac{1}{r}u_{r}\right)-\frac{1}{c_{\mathrm{L}}^{2}}\frac{\partial^{2}u_{r}}{\partial t^{2}}=0
}
$$
